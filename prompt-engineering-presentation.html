RULE 01

Be Clear

Provide complete context for your request

Why It Matters

AI can't read your mind. The more context you provide, the better the output. Include the purpose, constraints, and expected tone.

Be Clear: Examples

‚úóVague Prompt
"Write something about dogs."
‚úìClear Prompt
"Write a 200-word blog post about the benefits of adopting senior dogs, targeting families with children. Use a warm, encouraging tone."

Context Checklist

WHO
Who is the audience?
WHAT
What format & length?
HOW
What tone or style?
RULE 02

Explain Why

Give AI a role and purpose to guide its responses

Why It Matters

Defining a persona shapes the tone, depth, and expertise of responses. An AI acting as a "teacher" explains differently than one acting as a "peer."

Explain Why: Examples

‚úóNo Context
"Explain quantum computing."
‚úìRole Defined
"You are a patient science teacher explaining to a curious 12-year-old. Explain quantum computing using everyday analogies they can relate to."

RULE 04

Ask for Format

Specify exactly how you want the output structured

Why It Matters

Without format constraints, AI might give you an essay when you wanted bullet points, or 1000 words when you needed 100.

Ask for Format: Examples

‚úóNo Format
"Give me tips for better sleep."
‚úìFormat Specified
"Give me 5 tips for better sleep.
Format: numbered list, one sentence each, no introductions or conclusions."

RULE 05

Be Direct

Use step-by-step reasoning for complex tasks

Why It Matters

For complex problems, asking AI to "think step by step" produces more accurate, logical results. This is called Chain-of-Thought prompting.

Putting It Together

ROLE: You are a senior marketing consultant.
CONTEXT: I run a local coffee shop targeting remote workers.
EXAMPLE: For a similar bakery, you suggested partnering with co-working spaces.
FORMAT: Give me 3 strategies, each in 2-3 sentences.
STEPS: For each strategy, explain: the idea, why it works, and first action.
EDGE CASE: If you need more info about my budget, ask first.
CHAIN: After this, I'll ask you to expand my favorite into a full plan.
